About the Creator

Christopher Alexander serves as the band director and music director at Okeene Public Schools. His journey in musical arrangement began during his senior year at Putnam City West, where he arranged for the "Spirit of West" band. Now with sixteen award-winning marching shows to his credit, this original musical marks his debut as a musical theatre composer. As writer, composer, lyricist, and director, this production represents the culmination of his artistic vision and dedication to music education. The piece was crafted specifically to challenge students beyond their comfort zones, immersing them in the complex process of mounting a full-scale musical production.

To the Okeene administrative team and community for their continued support of the Okeene Fine Arts Department. Your commitment to arts education makes productions like this possible.
